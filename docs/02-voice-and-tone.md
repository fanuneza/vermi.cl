# vermi.cl Voice and Tone Guide

## Overall voice

The writing should feel like an experienced agronomist explaining vermicomposting to a curious neighbor.

It should be:

- calm
- confident
- practical
- curious
- precise
- approachable

Never sound like:

- marketing copy
- AI-generated content
- a university textbook
- clickbait
- an influencer

---

# Language

Use natural Chilean Spanish.

Always use "tú".

Avoid vocabulary that sounds distinctly Spanish, Argentine or Mexican unless there is no Chilean equivalent.

---

# Reading level

Write for an intelligent general audience.

Assume curiosity, not expertise.

Technical concepts are welcome, but every one must be explained.

---

# Introductions

Never start with generic filler.

Forbidden:

"Desde tiempos antiguos..."

"Cada día más personas..."

"En la actualidad..."

"Si llegaste hasta aquí..."

Begin by answering the question immediately.

---

# Explanations

Always explain why.

Bad:

Agrega cartón.

Good:

Agregar cartón aumenta la porosidad del sustrato y mejora el intercambio de oxígeno, reduciendo la actividad de microorganismos anaerobios.

---

# Tone

Encourage.

Do not reassure excessively.

Do not dramatize.

Do not exaggerate risks.

Do not oversimplify biology.

---

# Vocabulary

Prefer:

- lombrices
- lombrices californianas
- Eisenia fetida
- compostera
- vermicompostera
- sustrato
- residuos húmedos
- aportes verdes
- aportes cafés
- humus
- lixiviado
- té de humus

Avoid:

- gusanos
- bichos
- basura
- lecho
- cama
- humus líquido

Never call lixiviado "humus líquido."

---

# Titles

Use sentence case.

Correct:

Cómo alimentar una vermicompostera

Incorrect:

Cómo Alimentar Una Vermicompostera

Never place periods at the end of headings.

---

# Formatting

Prefer:

- short paragraphs
- descriptive headings
- tables
- bullet lists
- comparison boxes
- numbered procedures

Avoid walls of text.

---

# Scientific terminology

Introduce technical terms naturally.

Example:

Las lombrices no consumen directamente los restos de verduras. Se alimentan principalmente del biofilm microbiano que se desarrolla durante la descomposición.

---

# Reader relationship

Never talk down to the reader.

Assume mistakes come from lack of information, not lack of intelligence.

Treat every question as reasonable.

---

# Uncertainty

Differentiate between:

- established knowledge
- likely explanations
- unknowns

Never pretend certainty where none exists.

---

# Style checklist

Before publishing, verify:

- sounds human
- sounds Chilean
- avoids filler
- avoids repetition
- explains why
- uses consistent terminology
- flows naturally
- is pleasant to read
